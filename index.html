Your occasional back pain and belly fat are connected — both caused by weak core muscles from prolonged sitting. These exercises fix both simultaneously.
At-desk routine — every 90 minutes

Set a phone alarm every 90 min ⏰

Stand up and walk — 2–3 min around your floor. Non-negotiable.
Seated cat-cow stretch — arch and round your back 10 times slowly
Neck rolls — slow, controlled, 5 each side
Shoulder blade squeeze — pinch blades together, hold 5 sec, 10 reps
Hip flexor stretch — step one foot forward, push hips forward gently, 30 sec each side
10-min morning back routine — every day
Child's pose — 60 seconds, decompresses the spine
Cat-cow on all fours — 10 reps slow, morning mobility
Bird dog — 10 reps each side, strengthens deep core + lower back safely
Glute bridge — 3 × 15 reps, activates glutes which support the spine
Dead bug — 10 reps each side, safest core exercise for bad backs
Desk setup fixes — do today
Screen at eye level — stack books under laptop if needed
Sit with feet flat on floor, hips slightly above knees
Small rolled towel or lumbar cushion behind lower back
Phone alarm every 90 min as "stand up" reminder
Poor sleep + stress eating are the hidden #1 cause of belly fat. Cortisol (the stress hormone) directly signals your body to store fat around the belly. Fixing sleep will visibly accelerate your results.
Sleep protocol — target 7.5 hrs
Fixed sleep window: 10:30 PM → 6:00 AM — consistency matters more than duration
No screens 30 min before bed — switch to reading or stretching
Room temperature ~22–24°C and dark — improves sleep quality dramatically
No caffeine after 3 PM — it stays in your system for 6+ hours
1 glass warm turmeric milk at bedtime — aids recovery and sleep
Beating stress eating
Never sit hungry at your desk — keep roasted chana, almonds, or fruit at your desk always
The 10-minute rule — when a craving hits, drink water and wait 10 min. 70% of cravings pass
Don't stock junk at home — if it isn't there, you can't eat it at 11 PM
Eat every 3–4 hours — the meal plan prevents hunger spikes that cause stress eating
Box breathing — when stressed: 4 sec in, 4 hold, 4 out. Lowers cortisol fast
